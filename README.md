

# Onyx

**Automated expense tracking powered by Accessibility.**

Onyx captures your spending habits in real-time, directly from your payment apps, without the need for manual logging.

---

### Features

- **Automated Capture**: Automatically logs transactions from supported payment apps using Android Accessibility.
- **Privacy First**: Your data stays on your device. Local database, no analytics, no cloud sync.
- **Offline Core**: Works entirely offline for maximum speed and reliability.

### Tech Stack

- **Kotlin** & **Jetpack Compose**
- **Room** (Local Persistence)
- **DataStore** (Settings)

---

*Onyx is built for privacy-conscious users who want automated insights into their spending.*
